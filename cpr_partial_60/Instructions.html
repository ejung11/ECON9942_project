CPR game B: General Rules

  • The game consists of 20 rounds. But it could end early if group's total harvest exceed 100.
    • The probability of ending start from 50.5% when total harvest is 101, and increases 0.5% for a increase in total harvest.
    • ex) If total harvest is 110, the probability of ending is 55%. If total harvest is 150, the probability of ending is 75%.
  • The game will end immediately if total harvest exceeds 100 and the nature's power is less than the total harvest.
  • You will be given 25 efforts in every round and submit how much effort are you willing to harvest.
  • The return on harvesting is decided by total harvest of the group.
    • 23 - 0.25 * (Total Harvest)
  • The return on other work is 5.
  • You are putting 70% of your earnings from the harvest into group account.
  • You will receive equal share from group account.
  • Your earnings in the round is 0.3 {efforts harvested * (23 - 0.25 (Total Harvest) )} + 0.7{Group Account} + remaining efforts * 5
  • After everyone submitted, you will see your earnings.
