Rules
  • The game consists of 10 rounds.
  • You will be given 25 efforts in every round and will submit how many efforts to allocate to Activity B.
  • Sharing Rule
    • You will share 60% of your returns from Activity B with the other group members.
    • You will keep the remaining 40% of your returns from Activity B for yourself.
    • This means you will also receive an equal share of the group’s shared returns from Activity B.
    • You will not share any returns from Activity A.
  • The return on Activity B is determined by the group's total effort allocated to Activity B.
    • 20 - 0.1171 × (group's total effort allocated to Activity B) ECUs per effort
  • The return on Activity A is 5 ECUs per effort.
  • Your earnings for each round will be calculated as follows:
    • Earnings from Activity A
    • Earnings from Activity B
    • Earnings from Group's Sharing
  • After everyone has submitted their decisions, your earnings for the round will be shown to you.
