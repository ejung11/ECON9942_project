Settings

You will be playing the common-pool resource games. You can think of common-pool resource as firewood in forest, and offshore fishes. Anyone can come and harvest resource and resource is renewed each season.

In this session, imagine yourself as a fisherman fishing for fish or local villagers needing to find firewood. Fishes and firewood are common-pool resource, anyone can come and harvest as much as they want but it can be destroyed when people harvest too much.

You will be randomly assigned into group of 8 people. Think of the group as a village harvesting the common-pool resource. Other villages are so far away that you only interact with people in your group. Your payoff will be depends on your decision and others' decision in your group.
