Welcome

Thank you for participating in this experiment. Please make sure your cell phones are set to silence mode to avoid interruptions during the experiment.

In this experiment, you will be playing Common-pool resource games with two versions. Each version of CPR games will have one practice game before the real game.
In other words, you will be playing games in following order;
CPR game A: Practice, CPR game A: Real, CPR game B: Practice, CPR game B: Real.

You will be compensated with cash for participating the experiment. The payoff will be only depend on the real games you played; real CPR game A, and real CPR game B. At the end of the experiment, you will be paid one randomly between two games . A random draw will occur for each participant, deciding which games will be paid.
The tokens you earned in the game will be converted in USD at the following exchange rate:

100 Tokens = $ 1

If you read the instructions carefully and act wisely, you can earn up to $30. Your earning in this experiment depends both on your decision and the decision of others.

The session is expected to take 60 mins. If you have any questions, please raise your hand and wait for the experimenter to come and help you.
