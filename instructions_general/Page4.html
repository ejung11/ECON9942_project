Rounds & Destructions

The game can continue upto 20 rounds. But the game could end early if the resource is unable to renew.

After each round, the system will decide whether the resource has been destroyed or not depending on total harvest from the group. If the group harvested 100 efforts or less in total , the game will automatically continue to the next round.

But if the group harvested more than 100 efforts then the game could end early depend on the nature's power.
A random drawing between 0-200 will occur which will represent the regeneration power of the nature. If the drawn number is less than the total harvest , the game will end. In other words, the nature could not replenish the resource for the next round.

Each harvest after 100 will increase the probability of ending by 0.5% starting from 50%.
For example, if the group harvested 200 , then the probability of ending is 100% .
If the group harvested 150, then the probability of ending is 75%.
If the group harvested 100, then the probability of ending is 0%.
