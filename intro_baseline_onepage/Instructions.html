Random Matching and Anonymity

In this experiment, you will be randomly assigned to a group of 8 people. The group you are assigned to will stay the same throughout the entire experiment.

Settings

You will be playing an effort allocation game in a group of 8 people. In each round, you will decide how to allocate efforts between two different activities. Each round, you are endowed with 25 efforts and must decide how many to allocate to Activity A and Activity B.

  • Activity A: For each effort allocated to Activity A, you will receive a fixed return of 5 ECUs.
  • Activity B: The return on efforts allocated to Activity B is variable and depends on the total efforts allocated by the entire group, including yours.

    The return for each effort allocated to Activity B is:

    20 - 0.1171 × (group's total effort allocated to Activity B) ECUs per effort


Note that the return from Activity B decreases as the total efforts allocated to Activity B by the group increases.

Your earnings for each round will depend on both your own decisions and the decisions of others in your group.

Multiple Rounds

The game consists of 10 decision rounds. In each round, you will face the same decision task, where you will allocate efforts between Activity A and Activity B.

Earnings

After everyone has submitted their decisions, the earnings for the round will be calculated and shown to you.

  • Earnings from Activity A
  • Earnings from Activity B

Your final earnings will be the sum of your earnings across all 10 rounds.

Situation 1

This is round 1 of the game.
Assume the group's total allocation to Activity B is 60 efforts.

The return for each effort allocated to Activity B will be:
20 - 0.1171 × 60 = 12.97 ECUs per effort

If you allocate 10 efforts to Activity B, your earnings from Activity B will be:
10 × 12.97 = 129.7 ≈ 130 ECUs

For your remaining 15 efforts allocated to Activity A, your earnings from Activity A will be:
15 × 5 = 75 ECUs

Thus, your total earnings for this round will be:
130 + 75 = 205 ECUs

Situation 2

This is round 5 of the game.
Assume the group's total allocation to Activity B is 120 efforts.

The return for each effort allocated to Activity B will be:
20 - 0.1171 × 120 = 5.95 ECUs per effort

If you allocate 10 efforts to Activity B, your earnings from Activity B will be:
10 × 5.95 = 59.5 ≈ 60 ECUs

For your remaining 15 efforts allocated to Activity A, your earnings from Activity A will be:
15 × 5 = 75 ECUs

Thus, your total earnings for this round will be:
60 + 75 = 135 ECUs
