Random Matching and Anonymity

In this part, you will be randomly reassigned to a group of 8 people. The group you are assigned to will stay the same throughout Part 2.

Multiple Rounds

The game consists of 10 decision rounds.

Earnings

Your earnings in this part will be the sum of your earnings across all 10 rounds.

Settings

You will be playing an effort allocation game in a group of 8 people. In each round, you will decide how to allocate efforts between two different activities. Each round, you are endowed with 25 efforts and must decide how many to allocate to Activity A and Activity B.

New in this game
Sharing Rule
  • You will share 60% of your returns from Activity B with the other group members.
  • You will keep the remaining 40% of your returns from Activity B for yourself.
  • This means you will also receive an equal share of the group’s shared returns from Activity B.
  • You will not share any returns from Activity A.

So your earning in Part 2 is from Three sources.

  • Activity A: Fixed return of 5 ECUs per effort.
  • Activity B: Variable return depends on the total efforts allocated by the entire group, including yours.
    But, now you are only getting 40% from this activity. Sharing 60% of earnings from Activity B with your group members.

    The return for each effort allocated to Activity B is 40% of the following:

    20 - 0.1171 × (group's total effort allocated to Activity B) ECUs per effort

  • Group's Sharing: Equal share of group's shared return from Activity B.

Do Not Worry about the Calculation. We will provide you a tool for calculating your earnings.

Your earnings for each round will depend on both your own decisions and the decisions of others in your group, as well as the sharing rule for Activity B.

Situation 1

This is round 1 of the game.
Assume the group's total allocation to Activity B is 80 efforts.

The return for each effort allocated to Activity B will be:
20 - 0.1171 × 80 = 10.63 ECUs per effort

If you allocate 10 efforts to Activity B, your earnings from Activity B before sharing:
10 × 10.63 = 106.3 ECUs

Since You only keep 40% of your returns, your personal earnings from Activity B after sharing:
106.3 × 0.4 = 42.52 ≈ 43 ECUs

For your remaining 15 efforts allocated to Activity A, your earnings from Activity A will be:
15 × 5 = 75 ECUs

You will also receive 1/8 of the group's 60% share of Activity B returns:
(10.63 × 80) × 0.6 × 1/8 = 63.78 ≈ 64 ECUs

Thus, your total earnings for this round will be:
43 + 75 + 64 = 182 ECUs

Situation 2

This is round 2 of the game.
Assume the group's total allocation to Activity B is 150 efforts.

The return for each effort allocated to Activity B will be:
20 - 0.1171 × 150 = 2.44 ECUs per effort

If you allocate 10 efforts to Activity B, your earnings from Activity B will be:
10 × 2.44 = 24.4 ECUs

Since You only keep 40% of your returns, your personal earnings from Activity B after sharing:
24.4 × 0.4 = 9.76 ≈ 10 ECUs

For your remaining 15 efforts allocated to Activity A, your earnings from Activity A will be:
15 × 5 = 75 ECUs

You will also receive 1/8 of the group's 60% share of Activity B returns:
(2.44 × 150) × 0.6 × 1/8 = 27.45 ≈ 27 ECUs

Thus, your total earnings for this round will be:
10 + 75 + 27 = 112 ECUs
