This is the practice of CPR game B.

In this session, imagine yourself as a fisherman fishing for fish or local villagers needing to find firewood. Fishes and firewood are common-pool resource, anyone can come and harvest as much as they want but it can be destroyed when people harvest too much.

But the following is new in this game:

  • Now you are sharing 70% of earnings from harvest with the people in your group.
  • This means that you will also receive equal amount from the group.
  • You will not be sharing the earning from other work.
