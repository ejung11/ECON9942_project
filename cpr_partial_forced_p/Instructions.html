CPR game B: General Rules

  • The game consists of 20 rounds. But it could end early if group's total harvest exceed 100.
  • You will be given 25 efforts in every round and submit how much effort are you willing to harvest.
  • After everyone submitted, you will see your earnings.
  • The game will end immediately if total harvest exceeds 100 and the nature's power is less than the total harvest.
